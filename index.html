Questions

How fast will I get my guide?

You'll receive your 8-page guide within 3-5 business days after our call. If you're in a rush, let me know when you book and I'll do my best to speed things up.

What if I can't find the wines you recommend?

Every recommendation comes with two backup alternatives. I also include a list of reliable retailers, so you'll always have options.

Do you work with any budget?

Yes. Whether you want great value or special-occasion bottles, I'll work within your range. Just tell me your budget during our call.

Can you help with beer or cocktails too?

My focus is wine, but I'm happy to give general guidance on other drinks during our call. The guide itself covers wine only.

What if I'm not happy with the guide?

Let me know within 7 days and I'll revise it or give you a full refund. Simple as that.
