Your Sommelier

I'm Anthony. Some of my earliest memories are sitting at wine tastings with my family. That early exposure turned into a lifelong passion that's taken me around the world chasing great bottles and the stories behind them.

I got my WSET certification because I wanted to do this properly. After helping friends pick wines for their weddings and watching them stress over choices they shouldn't have to make alone, I built CellarSense.

Whether you're planning a 30-person backyard dinner or a 200-guest reception, I'll help you find wines that work for your taste, your menu, and your budget.

Here's the thing: wine is supposed to be fun. Tasty, interesting, close to the earth. It doesn't have to be scary or snobbish. I'm here to be the bridge.
